## What is VPN?

**VPN (Virtual Personal Network)** is a type of connection you can use to secure your identity and protect your personal information when surfing the Internet. VPN creates a secure “tube” to connect your Android device to the Internet, which means all the activities on your Android device will be encrypted. So hackers cannot steal your personal information, and your privacy also won’t be revealed to your government and your ISP (Internet Service Provider).
