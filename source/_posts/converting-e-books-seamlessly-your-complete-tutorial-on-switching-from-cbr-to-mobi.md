As we all know Kindle devices and related software are good tools to read comic. ![CBR TO MOBI](http://www.epubor.com/images/uppic/CBR-TO-MOBI.png)The disappointing fact is that CBR is not supported by Kindle. #### Step 2: Adding CBR Files to Calibre

After installing Calibre, Select CBR files, drag and drop them to Calibre interface. Or you can click "Add books" button in the navigation bar.

![Calibre adding books](http://www.epubor.com/images/uppic/calibre-adding-books.png)

#### Step 3: Convert CBR Files to Mobi

Select CBR files and right click it. You can see convert button in tap control. Of course, you can also select "Convert books" button in top navigation bar. From displayed options, bulk convert option can batch convert files quickly. Choose the suitable option right for you.

![select right convert option](http://www.epubor.com/images/uppic/select-right-convert-option.png)

Select Mobi as your output format and click OK. Then you can see the converting progress at the bottom of the interface. When the "Jobs" record turns run "0", you can find the converted Mobi books in your local Calibre Library of your PC.

![cbr to mobi format](http://www.epubor.com/images/uppic/cbr-to-mobi-format.png)
