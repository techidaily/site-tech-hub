### Method 3: Use PDFelement

**PDFelement** is a user-friendly PDF editor that is available for **Windows** , **Mac** and **iOS** . It is small in size and it is easy to use. With PDFelement, you can get everything you need to do with PDF documents.

 It just takes several clicks to split PDF documents with PDFelement. The following steps show you how to do it on Windows platform. The steps on macOS is similar.

 1.[**Download**](http://download.wondershare.com/pdfelement6-pro%5Ffull2990.exe?%5Fga=2.24434681.231448722.1551859212-987438601.1550800969) and install PDFelement.

 2\. Run PDFelement. Click**Edit PDF** .

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/03/image-107-1024x524.png)

 3\. In the pop-up window, locate the PDF document you want to split, then**double-click** on it to open in PDFelement.

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/03/image-108.png)

 4\. Navigate to**the Page tab** , select the pages you want to split,**right-click** on it, then select**Extract Page** .
