### Method 1: Block trackers with the Content Blocking feature in Firefox

 With the Content Blocking feature, you can create a tracker-free zone when surfing the web with Firefox. To adjust the Content Blocking feature in Firefox:

 1) In Firefox, click**the menu button** on the upper-right corner, then select**Options** .

### Method 3: Block pop-up windows for specific websites

 You can also block pop-up windows for specific websites in Firefox. Here is how to do it:

1) Open the website you want to block ads in Firefox.

2) Click**the lock icon** next to**the address bar** in Firefox, then click**the gear icon** next to**Permissions** .
