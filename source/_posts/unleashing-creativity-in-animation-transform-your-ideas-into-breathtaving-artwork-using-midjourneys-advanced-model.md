##  Making Cool Anime Art With the Right Keywords

 If you ask Niji to generate something without specifying that it should be anime style, you'll get similar results to the main model, if not slightly worse. What you ask for in your prompt is still quite important. As an example, here are some important things to include in your prompt depending on what you want:

* The words "anime" or "manga."
* The era of anime.
* Specific show names if you want to emulate that style.
* The gender, race, or job of a character. E.g. "elf", "warrior", samurai. etc.
* The actions or poses characters are taking.
